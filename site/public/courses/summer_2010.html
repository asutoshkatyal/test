Philosophy 100D
|
 Philosophy of Mind
|
 BLDG 387 RM 101| Session B (TWR 12:30pm-1:55pm) + Discussion
Discussion of some central questions about the mind: are people identical to their bodies? What is it to feel, believe, or desire something? What distinguishes intelligent thinking from a computer's activities? What is the connection between language and thought?

Required Text(s)

Block, ed., Readings in Philosophy of Psychology, Vol. 1 (Harvard University Press) ISBN-13: 978-0674748767
Kim, Philosophy of Mind (Westview Press, 2005) ISBN-13: 9780813342696
